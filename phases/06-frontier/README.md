# Phase 6: Frontier Research

**Duration:** Ongoing
**Prerequisites:** Phases 1-5
**Capstone:** Original research contribution (paper, tool, or significant PR)

## Objectives

By the end of this phase, you will:
- Read and critically evaluate arXiv quantum computing papers
- Reproduce published results with your own implementations
- Identify open problems and potential improvements
- Produce original research or a significant contribution

## Approach

### Paper Reproduction (Weeks 1-4)
- Select 3+ recent papers from arXiv quant-ph
- Criteria: clear methodology, available data, implementable algorithms
- For each paper:
  1. Summarize key claims and methods
  2. Implement the algorithm/protocol
  3. Reproduce main results
  4. Compare your results to published figures
  5. Write up analysis (agreements, discrepancies, insights)

### Original Research (Weeks 5+)
- Identify a gap, improvement, or novel application
- Design experiments and implement solution
- Benchmark against existing approaches
- Write up results in paper format
- Consider: arXiv preprint, blog post, or conference submission

## Research Areas of Interest

(To be refined as learning progresses)
- Variational algorithm improvements
- Error mitigation techniques
- Quantum simulation of physical systems
- Quantum-classical hybrid architectures
- Novel applications of existing algorithms

## Resources

- arXiv quant-ph (daily)
- Google Scholar alerts
- Quantum computing conference proceedings (QIP, TQC)
- Research group blogs (IBM, Google, AWS, Microsoft)
